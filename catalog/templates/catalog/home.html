Home page!

To use the collectio website, you will need to login with your username and password. If you would like to become an administrator, you will need to contact the creators of the app.

To register as a user, you can sign up on the register page by clicking the button on the top-left. After logging in with your ID, you will be ready to work with your catalog system. Collectio allows it's users to create catalogs with records of any type. The records can have various fields like:

  • Name and Description
  • Acquisition and Creation Year
  • Manufacturer
  • Condition Rating and Summary
  • Provenance History
  • Image
  • And other custom fields like Color

The app also allows you to create, view, search, edit and delete catalogs, records and even the individual fields. We hope to keep improving this website. The code for the wesbite can be found here: Github.
